About Victor

Ogunlusi Victor is a student of the university of Lagos, currently working towards graduating as a computer scientist. He is currently in 400l. He is hoping to have a future in the software engineering industry and also the web development world.

Victor attended mbari mbayo primary school. From there he went to zeekay college where he finished as the second best graduating student. During his school life, he was always among the top three students. He won awards on neatness and was the headprefect in secondary school.He was a strong advocate of order, decency and good organization.

Victor is an awardee of the NNPC/Total schorlaship scheme. He has also been awarded twice in the unilag endowment programme. He has good knowledge in visual basic, java and c# and is currently improving his web skills.

In his spare time, Victor enjoys programming, gaming, watching movies or animes. He also enjoys spending time with family and friends.
